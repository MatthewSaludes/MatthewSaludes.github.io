Who is the Author?

Hello, I am Matthew C. Saludes and I am a Grade 9 16-year-old scholar from PSHS-CLC. I created this website not only for my project but also to gauge not just my own understanding about the Universe but yours as well. I am also interested in Astronomy as well as the Physics of the Universe. Here in PSHS, we are taught about these things and I am interested in sharing these topics to you. In the Philippines, we do not have much people interested that much in Astronomy. I hope, with this website, to encourage you to be able to understand more about the Universe and its contents and to be able to promote Astronomy in the R & D Department of the Philippines. It is, to my interest, that the Philippines take an active role in the development of space technology.
