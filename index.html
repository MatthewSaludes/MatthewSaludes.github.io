What is Astronomer?

Astronomer is a free website that offers to showcase the contents of the Universe to people who are also interested in Astronomy. It highlights the most commonly looked topic about the Universe from the Earth to Blackholes and also offers interesting topics to readers.

Pictures and Descriptions


The Astronomer provides accurate descriptions and pictures on the topic and about the Universe and also offers sources.

Interactives


The Astronomer also provides some interactive projects to showcase a glimpse of the Universe's time periods and contents and to give the reader a chance to explore the Universe.

Recommended sources


Here in Astronomer, we provide sources for more information about space on other websites and in Youtube in order to provide better understanding on our Universe.
